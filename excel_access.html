How We Do It

At EcoVoyage, we harness the power of technology and sustainability to create unforgettable, eco-friendly travel experiences.

We use Microsoft Access to manage client bookings and ensure seamless itineraries. Excel helps us maintain detailed reports of costs, revenue, and carbon-offsetting contributions. Every detail, from the number of travelers to destination-specific data, is tracked to provide insights for improving our operations while minimizing our ecological footprint.

Our operations team, led by Michael Vick, analyzes Excel reports to track which travel packages are most popular and align them with our sustainability goals. This data-driven approach ensures that we continue to offer trips that leave a positive impact on the environment and local communities.

Delivery Policies

Upon booking, all travel documents, itineraries, and e-tickets are delivered electronically via email to reduce paper waste. EcoVoyage is committed to supporting a digital-first approach for all communications, ensuring a faster, greener delivery process. Customers can also access their trip details through our secure online portal.

Return Policies

We understand that plans can change. EcoVoyage offers a flexible return policy for cancellations or modifications.

  • Cancellations made 30 days or more before the departure date are eligible for a full refund.
  • Cancellations made 15-29 days before the departure date are eligible for a 50% refund.
  • Cancellations made less than 15 days before the departure date are non-refundable but can be credited toward a future trip.

Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days. For modifications, please contact our customer service team, and we will do our best to accommodate your request.
