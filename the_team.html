Meet the EcoVoyage Team

Chris Brown - Founder & CEO

Chris Brown is the visionary behind EcoVoyage. With a deep passion for sustainable travel, Chris founded EcoVoyage to inspire travelers to explore the world responsibly. Under his leadership, EcoVoyage has pioneered eco-friendly travel packages that prioritize local communities and minimize environmental impact.

Cole Palmer - Sustainability Specialist

Cole Palmer is the driving force behind EcoVoyage’s green initiatives. With a background in environmental science and years of experience in sustainability consulting, Cole ensures that every trip curated by EcoVoyage adheres to eco-friendly practices. From carbon offsetting to promoting renewable energy in destinations, Cole's work keeps EcoVoyage true to its mission.

Michael Vick - Head of Customer Experience

Michael Vick brings a wealth of experience in hospitality and customer service to EcoVoyage. His focus is on creating unforgettable, sustainable travel experiences for every client. Michael’s dedication to detail ensures that each itinerary not only delights travelers but also contributes positively to local communities.

Join us in exploring the world responsibly. Together, we can make travel a force for good! #SustainableTravel #EcoVoyage
